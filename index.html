Pet Ball

Dispenser

Our Created Device, the Pet Ball Dispenserfor the lack of names, is a product that dispenses regular sized ping pong balls when a user shines a laser on the device's photocell or uses the built-in multifunction knob
All for the enjoyment of a pet such as a kitten 😸
